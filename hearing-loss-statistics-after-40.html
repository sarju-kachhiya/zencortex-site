What the Data Shows

Why Hearing Changes Begin After 40

Age-related hearing changes are often linked to:

  • Reduced microcirculation to the inner ear
  • Oxidative stress affecting auditory hair cells
  • Nerve signaling inefficiencies
  • Prolonged exposure to environmental noise

The inner ear contains extremely delicate structures that depend on consistent oxygen and nutrient supply. Even minor changes in blood flow may influence auditory clarity.
